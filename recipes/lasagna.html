World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is the purported world's best lasagna by John Chandler. It requires 30 minutes of prep time. The cook time is 2 hours and 30 minutes. This recipe serves 12.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna ingredients
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
